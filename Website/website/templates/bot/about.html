What is Vipera Veil?

We are a competitive Star Citizen Clan focused on PVP. Jump on our voice channels, hang out with us and get to know us! If you are interested in joining us please fill in an application.


We are looking for:

  • Devotion to the group
  • Loyalty
  • Respect
  • Positivity
  • Trustworthy individuals
  • Knowledge of the game
  • Good humor
  • Will to get better/improve
We are the competitive clan of warriors following the traditions of the Mandalorian from the Star Wars universe. within our team culture we follow the creed to not only create a organization but to create a lifelong bond and family.

The Creed.

Strength is life
for the strong will have the right to rule
honor is life for with no honor one may as well be dead
loyalty is life
for without ones clan, one has no purpose
death is life
one should die as they lived.

Roles:

Pilots
    We are looking for team focused pilots who want to sharpen their skills and learn to fight as one. The correct attitude is the most important. If you bring a willingness to learn, we will teach you the way.
FPS
    We are looking for people who work well solo, but also are willing to work as a team for target exploitation and infiltration. Proper attitude and a growth mindset required.
Specialists
    We also welcome players who want to explore other aspects of Star Citizen. Anything from mining and hauling to low flying.
